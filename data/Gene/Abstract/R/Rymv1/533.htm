基因(座)名称稻黄矮病毒抗性基因  rice yellow mottle virus resistance 1
基因符号Rymv1;
所在染色体12 （未克隆）
供体材料Tog5672(非洲栽培稻)
Rymv1 locus, controlling the high recessive resistance to rice yellow mottle virus (RYMV), was mapped to a 160-kb interval containing a gene from the elF(iso)4G family. Transformation of a resistant line with a elF(iso)4G cDNA, isolated from a susceptile variety, resulted in the loss of resistance in stable transgenic plants. Analysis of Rymv1 allelic variability from susceptible and resistant varieties show amino acid change in the MIF4G domain of elf(iso)4G translation initiation factor confers high resistance to RYMV, this suggests that this domain may be involved in the interaction with the virus.
